Welcome to our Restaurant

Catering is a critical component of your event, so we take the same care with our catered meals as we do in our restaurant. Serving the highest quality of food and providing superior service, each dish uses the freshest ingredients and spices imported exclusively from India. Custom equipment made to our specifications ensures we deliver the honest flavors of India and remain faithful to the family recipes handed down for generations. Since 1998, Golden Grill has been serving authentic Indian food in the Denver Metro Area and our chefs, who began their careers in Northern India, have mastered the art of preparing classic Indian cuisine.
